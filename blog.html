Site Report

Getting a start

There have been a lot of things which I have needed to sort out before I got started with my website such as setting up my git and github and thinking about what I want my website to look like. In terms of look for now I have decided to go for a dark theme and so when I started to make my homepage I have just made the background black and the text white to get an idea. I've spent a lot of time looking at the page and just thought the darker theme centered around black would not work. Starting has been hard and at this point I can not see how the front page will look at all and this is a problem as I do think I can't continue with the progress of the other pages if I don't figure out what I do with the front page. For now I just decided to do rough templates of the other pages and forget about the design.

Paddings, borders and steady progress with design

As I have been focusing with basic layout more and more and looking where to position things on the page padding has been one of the elements which I have used throughout my all pages and one that has taken me a long time to get the result I wanted and I am constantly changing the value of my paddings if I change one thing or if a position just does not look right. I've used padding for moving my text to be positioned more at centre or to push text vertically down. Whilst progressing with my pages design wise for pages with columns I've really wanted the columns to have soft round edges opposed to the sharp edged boxes and so I have been using the border-radius as they seem more easy to follow. Speaking of designs, I have made some steady progress with the design in terms of colour. The palette which I have thought of has constantly changed from me wanting a black and purple palette to a pink and brown palette to a blue and green palette to then a mixture of the various paletes in mind. Choosing and sticking to colours is not an easy task and it was hard to envision what the pages would turn out to be as I just had so many colours in my head but after a while I tried again with my home page and can finally see that my main colour was going to be a green and so I can kind of see how my other pages will look like.
